FRUITS

A fruit is a developed ovary of a plant that comes from a flower and contains one or more multiple seeds.

fruits are categorized into two:

  • Fleshy fruits
  • Dry fruits

    • Fleshy fruits

    They comprise a fleshy outer layer within a leathery skin.They include:

    1. Blueberry
    2. Cucumber
    3. Mulberry
    4. Brinjal

    dry fruits

    They are fruits that have been dried either naturally or artificially.they include:

    1. raisins
    2. plums
    3. figs
    4. apricots
